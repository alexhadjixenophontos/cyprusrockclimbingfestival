WHAT IS THE CYPRUS ROCK CLIMBING FESTIVAL (CRCF)?

The Cyprus Rock Climbing Festival (CRCF) is an annual celebration of climbing, adventure, and community held in the picturesque village of Ineia, nestled in the Paphos region of Cyprus.

Attracting climbers, outdoor enthusiasts, and industry professionals from around the globe, the festival showcases the exceptional limestone terrain of the region while promoting sustainability, supporting local businesses, and celebrating the vibrant outdoor culture of Cyprus.

KEY CRCF FIGURES:

  • 3-day festival featuring diverse activities and cultural experiences
  • 400+ attendees from Cyprus and abroad
  • 40+ climbing & outdoor workshops led by certified instructors
  • 3 competitions (including 2 climbing and 2 photography competitions)
  • Live music, games and a special rock painting workshop in the Inia village square
  • Hiking and orienteering activities
  • High-level participation, including the Minister of Agriculture and local authorities
  • Multiple presentations & panels on environmental awareness and climbing culture

Accessibility at the CRCF

Got an accessibility concern or request? Reach out to us at cyprusrockclimbingfestival@gmail.com—we're here to listen and support however we can.

We're committed to making the Cyprus Rock Climbing Festival a more inclusive and welcoming experience. While we may not be able to accommodate every request, we'll always be honest about our venues, staff capabilities, and the nature of our activities.

Thanks for helping us build a more accessible climbing community

ABOUT

MISSION

Our mission is to develop, protect and celebrate rock climbing in Cyprus.

All proceeds from the festival will go towards the maintenance of the Cyprus climbing crags particularly in Ineia/Droushia. This will be a not-for-profit festival.

Our goal is to:

  • Celebrate the sport of rock climbing
  • Promote sustainable climbing
  • Build vibrant community spirit
  • Support local community development
  • Ensure a positive environmental impact
  • Promote Cyprus as a climbing destination

TEAM

ORGANIZER

Active Zone Outdoor

Active Zone Outdoor is a Cyprus based non for profit, non-governmental organisation established in Larnaca in 2019, having as a mission to connect young people with opportunities to develop better mental health through sports and outdoor activities and be actively engaged in society achieving personal fulfillment.

Active Zone Outdoor as the Organiser, fully supports the mission of the festival, since among others, the organisation aims at promoting the sport of rock climbing and its benefits to young people facilitating the development of mental and physical health and their connection with the physical environment, achieving better environmental awareness, healthy lifestyle and well-being.

Striving towards the promotion and cultivation of inclusive communities, Active Zone Outdoor exploits rock climbing as an intervention tool for social inclusion as well as provides accessibility to young people with fewer opportunities, those coming from vulnerable backgrounds and people with disabilities.

Socials: @activezoneoutdoor, Active Zone Outdoor

From Nicosia, Larnaca, or Limassol

Take an Intercity Bus to Paphos Karavella Bus Station:

  • Nicosia → Paphos: ~2.5 hours
  • Larnaca → Paphos: ~2 hours
  • Limassol → Paphos: ~1 hour 15 minutes

Schedules available at intercity-buses.com.

From Paphos to Ineia

From Karavella Station, take Bus 641 to Ineia Square 1:

  • Operates Monday–Saturday
  • Departure: 12:00 and 15:20
  • Arrival: 12:54 and 16:02
  • Duration: ~55 minutes

Route details: BusMaps Cyprus – Route 641

By Car to Ineia

  • From Nicosia: ~185 km / ~2.5 hrs via A1 → A6 → B7 → E709
  • From Larnaca: ~165 km / ~2.25 hrs via A5 → A1 → A6 → B7 → E709
  • From Limassol: ~115 km / ~1.75 hrs via A6 → B7 → E709
  • From Paphos: ~35 km / ~45 mins via B7 → E709

Roads are paved; expect winding sections near Ineia. Parking available near the square.
