CRCF 2024 - A SUCCESSFUL FIRST YEAR!

October 26-28, 2024

WHAT WAS THE CYPRUS ROCK CLIMBING FESTIVAL 2024?

The first Cyprus Rock Climbing Festival (CRCF) was an annual celebration of climbing, adventure, and community held in the picturesque village of Ineia, nestled in the Paphos region of Cyprus.

Attracting climbers, outdoor enthusiasts, and industry professionals from around the globe, the festival showcased the exceptional limestone terrain of the region while promoting sustainability, supporting local businesses, and celebrating the vibrant outdoor culture of Cyprus.

CRCF 2024 KEY FIGURES:

  • 3-day festival featuring diverse activities and cultural experiences
  • 400+ attendees from Cyprus and abroad
  • 40+ climbing & outdoor workshops led by certified instructors
  • 3 competitions (including climbing and photography competitions)
  • Live music, games and a special rock painting workshop in the Ineia village square
  • Hiking and orienteering activities
  • High-level participation, including the Minister of Agriculture and local authorities
  • Multiple presentations & panels on environmental awareness and climbing culture

Accessibility at the CRCF 2024

We were committed to making the Cyprus Rock Climbing Festival a more inclusive and welcoming experience. We offered free climbing workshops for families, youth with disabilities, and underrepresented groups.

Activities promoted equal access to the sport, social interaction, and the physical and mental benefits of climbing.

ABOUT CRCF 2024

MISSION

Our mission was to develop, protect and celebrate rock climbing in Cyprus.

All proceeds from the festival went towards the maintenance of the Cyprus climbing crags particularly in Ineia/Droushia. This was a not-for-profit festival.

Our goals were to:

  • Celebrate the sport of rock climbing
  • Promote sustainable climbing
  • Build vibrant community spirit
  • Support local community development
  • Ensure a positive environmental impact
  • Promote Cyprus as a climbing destination

TEAM

ORGANIZER

Active Zone Outdoor

Active Zone Outdoor is a Cyprus based non for profit, non-governmental organisation established in Larnaca in 2019, having as a mission to connect young people with opportunities to develop better mental health through sports and outdoor activities and be actively engaged in society achieving personal fulfillment.

Active Zone Outdoor as the Organiser, fully supports the mission of the festival, since among others, the organisation aims at promoting the sport of rock climbing and its benefits to young people facilitating the development of mental and physical health and their connection with the physical environment, achieving better environmental awareness, healthy lifestyle and well-being.

Striving towards the promotion and cultivation of inclusive communities, Active Zone Outdoor exploits rock climbing as an intervention tool for social inclusion as well as provides accessibility to young people with fewer opportunities, those coming from vulnerable backgrounds and people with disabilities.

Socials: @activezoneoutdoor, Active Zone Outdoor
