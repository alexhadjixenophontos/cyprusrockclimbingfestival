CRCF 2024 Festival Schedule

Experience 3 days of climbing, community, and adventure in the beautiful village of Ineia, Paphos.

Saturday, October 26, 2024

08:30 - 17:00
Registration Desk

Get information about workshops, climbing competition, photography competition, game registration, etc.

10:00 - 13:00
Climbing Workshops - Morning Session

Intro to Bouldering, Multipitch, Sport Climbing, Trad Climbing, Abseil/Rappelling, Top Rope Climbing

14:00 - 17:00
Climbing Workshops - Afternoon Session

Advanced techniques and specialized climbing skills

18:00 - 20:00
Evening Presentations & Entertainment

Welcome Speeches, Fontainebleau Experience, Climbing Guidebooks, IFSC & Comp Climbing, Meteora Adventure, Short Climbing Movies

Sunday, October 27, 2024

09:00 - 12:00
Competition Games

Quick Draw a Quickdraw, Clip & Rip, Coil & Conquer, Boulder Burpees, Pull up Challenge, Hang Challenge

14:00 - 17:00
Special Programs

Climbing for people with disabilities, Climb with your Kids!, Top Rope Climbing for Young People

19:00 - 22:00
Live Music & Entertainment

Valentinos Dimitriou (Cypriot/Greek music), Vasiliki Anastasiou & Ermis Michail (Traditional with a twist)

Monday, October 28, 2024

09:00 - 12:00
Photography Competition

Capture the action and beauty of the CRCF. Submit for Best Action Shot and/or Best Scenic Shot to win prizes!

14:00 - 17:00
Cultural Activities & Closing

Stone painting workshops, hiking "Aphrodite" route, festival closing ceremony

Festival Highlights:

  • Climbing Competitions: Sport climbing and bouldering competitions
  • Photography Competition: Best Action Shot and Best Scenic Shot categories
  • Orienteering Competition: Organized by OrientAction
  • Climbing Workshops: Bouldering, Multipitch, Sport Climbing, Trad Climbing, Abseil/Rappelling
  • Special Programs: Climbing for people with disabilities, Kids climbing, Youth programs
  • Live Music: Cypriot and Greek traditional music with modern twists
  • Cultural Activities: Stone painting workshops, hiking "Aphrodite" route
  • Environmental Focus: Responsible mountain behavior presentations
  • Fun Games: Quick Draw, Clip & Rip, Boulder Burpees, Pull up Challenge, Hang Challenge
